Towards Trustworthy Deployment

Medical Imaging · Interpretability · Trustworthy AI
TL;DR

A practical checklist to audit medical AI before deployment.

The Problem

Accuracy alone is not enough for hospitals. We need a lightweight audit toolkit.

Our Approach

Based on other threads, we draft a 5-step checklist: 1) Dataset shortcut scan, 2) Grad-CAM sanity check, 3) External validation, 4) Human alignment rating, 5) Failure mode documentation. We test the checklist on our own models.

What We Found

Checklist caught 2 shortcut issues we missed initially. We built a one-page Streamlit app where you upload model + image and it outputs attention map + alignment score. Next step: share with mentors for feedback.

Why It Matters / Open Questions

If models rely on shortcuts, high accuracy is misleading. Next: test with external hospital data, compare Grad-CAM vs Score-CAM, and build a simple audit checklist clinicians can use before deployment.

References

Selvaraju et al. Grad-CAM, Geirhos et al. Shortcut Learning, Adebayo et al. Sanity Checks.
